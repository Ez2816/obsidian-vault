## Upload queue

1. BMEG 101 and 102 — highest value for verifying the PBME-specific biomedical and laboratory foundation.
2. CHEM 121 and 123 — verify molecular structure, thermodynamics, kinetics, stereochemistry, and laboratory depth.
3. PHYS 157, 158, and 170 — verify the physics foundation feeding thermodynamics, circuits, imaging, and biomechanics.
4. MATH 100, 101, and 152 — verify notation, applications, computational laboratories, and prerequisite bridges.
5. APSC 160 — verify programming language, acquisition hardware, simulation, and data-processing expectations.
6. APSC 100 and WRDS 150 — verify design, ethics, sustainability, graphics, research writing, and communication artifacts.

## Naming and overlap decisions

- Existing second-year concept pages were linked rather than duplicated where the conceptual scope substantially overlapped.
- New pages focus on first-year foundations or PBME-specific laboratory contexts.
- The unspecified English alternative is not treated as a named completed course.

## Future source-ingestion rule

For each uploaded course source: identify the course and related seed pages; preserve useful intuition; compare every substantive claim with the original; add exact terminology, equations with defined variables, diagrams, laboratories, and examples; cite the Source path; update source status and confidence; record conflicts; update this tracker and the index; append a system-log entry; and report every changed file. Do not replace an intuitive explanation merely because the source is more formal—retain both levels when accurate.
