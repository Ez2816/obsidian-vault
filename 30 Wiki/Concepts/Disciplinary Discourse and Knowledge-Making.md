## Common reasoning mistakes

- Memorizing terminology without identifying the represented system or measurable consequence.
- Ignoring units, direction, reference conditions, uncertainty, or domain restrictions.
- Treating an idealized relationship as exact outside its assumptions.
- Confusing a model prediction with experimental or clinical evidence.
