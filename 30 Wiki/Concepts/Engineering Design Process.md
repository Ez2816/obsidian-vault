# Engineering Design Process

## Plain-language overview

Engineering Design Process is a foundational idea in BMEG 257. In plain language, it provides a disciplined way to describe, predict, evaluate, or communicate part of a biomedical engineering system. The important move is to identify what is being represented, what assumptions make the representation useful, and what evidence would show that it is inadequate.
