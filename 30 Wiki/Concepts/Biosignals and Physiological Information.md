# Biosignals and Physiological Information

## Plain-language overview

A biosignal is a measurable quantity related to biological activity, but interpretation depends on acquisition conditions, noise, variability, and the physiological model.

The practical question is not only “what does this term mean?” but also “what does it let us predict, measure, construct, or decide?” A useful explanation therefore connects the idea to observable quantities, units or categories, assumptions, and limits.
