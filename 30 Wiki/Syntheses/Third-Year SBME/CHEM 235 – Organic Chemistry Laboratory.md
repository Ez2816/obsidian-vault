# CHEM 235 – Organic Chemistry Laboratory

## Official scope

CHEM 235 is a 1-credit laboratory course in organic-chemistry techniques, taken with or after CHEM 233.

## Main course content

The official description does not enumerate individual experiments. A cautious seed scope includes:

- Safe handling of organic reagents and solvents
- Measurement, reaction setup, and controlled heating or cooling
- Extraction and phase separation
- Filtration and drying
- Recrystallization or other purification methods
- Chromatographic separation
- Product isolation and yield calculation
- Basic characterization and purity assessment
- Laboratory notebook and procedural documentation
- Waste segregation and chemical safety

These techniques are standard possibilities, not confirmed claims about the exact CHEM 235 experiment sequence.

## Prerequisite context

The official prerequisite is one of CHEM 123, CHEM 135, or SCIE 001, with CHEM 233 or CHEM O 213 as a corequisite. Credit exclusions apply with CHEM 203.
