# CHEM 233 – Organic Chemistry for the Biological Sciences

## Official scope

CHEM 233 is a 3-credit course on the reactions and properties of carbonyl compounds, carbohydrates, amino acids, and nucleic acids.

## Main course content

- Carbonyl structure, polarity, and reactivity
- Aldehydes and ketones
- Carboxylic acids and their derivatives
- Nucleophilic addition and substitution reasoning
- Structure and reactions of carbohydrates
- Stereochemistry in biologically relevant molecules
- Amino-acid structure, ionization, and reactivity
- Peptide-bond formation and hydrolysis
- Nucleobases, nucleosides, and nucleotides
- Chemical structure of nucleic acids
- Mechanistic reasoning using electron movement

## Prerequisite context

The official prerequisite is one of CHEM 123, CHEM 130, or SCIE 001. Credit exclusions apply with CHEM 203 and CHEM 223.
