# BMEG 357 – Biomedical Engineering Design II

## Official scope

BMEG 357 is a 3-credit supervised biomedical-design course focused on creating biomedical-technology innovations for clinical clients. Students build, test, and verify prototypes with potential users.

## Main course content

- Defining a clinical design problem
- Working with clinical clients and potential users
- Translating observations and needs into design requirements
- Generating and selecting concepts
- Planning a supervised design process
- Building functional prototypes
- Designing tests and acceptance criteria
- Verifying prototypes against requirements
- Collecting structured feedback from potential users
- Iterating from test evidence
- Maintaining design documentation and traceability

## Prerequisite context

The official prerequisite is BMEG 257.
