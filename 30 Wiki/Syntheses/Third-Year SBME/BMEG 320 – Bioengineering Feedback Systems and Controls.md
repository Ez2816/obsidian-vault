# BMEG 320 – Bioengineering Feedback Systems and Controls

## Official scope

BMEG 320 is a 3-credit course covering the modelling, analysis, and simulation of dynamic systems. Its official scope includes state-space modelling, linearization, feedback, stability, sensitivity, and control-system design, with examples from biological systems, assistive systems, and biomedical equipment.

## Main course content

- Dynamic-system variables, inputs, outputs, and state
- Differential-equation and state-space models
- Equilibrium points and operating conditions
- Linearization of nonlinear systems
- Open-loop and closed-loop behaviour
- Feedback-system structure
- Stability analysis
- Sensitivity to parameters and disturbances
- Transient and steady-state response
- Control-system performance criteria
- Controller design and simulation
- Biological, assistive-technology, and biomedical-equipment examples

## Prerequisite context

The official prerequisite is either BMEG 220 or the specified ELEC 221/201/202 pathway.
