# NURS 180 – Stress and Strategies to Promote Well Being

## Official scope

NURS 180 is a 3-credit introduction to foundational knowledge about stress, stressors, resilience, social support, and strategies that foster physical, mental, emotional, and spiritual health.

## Main course content

- Definitions and models of stress
- Internal and external stressors
- Physiological and psychological stress responses
- Acute and chronic stress
- Individual differences in stress appraisal and response
- Resilience and protective factors
- Social support and interpersonal resources
- Physical-health strategies
- Mental- and emotional-health strategies
- Spiritual dimensions of well-being
- Evaluating coping strategies and their limitations
- Navigating stressors associated with student life

## Prerequisite context

The official description states that the course is open to students interested in managing and navigating student-life stressors; no prerequisite is listed.
