# BIOC 202 – Introductory Medical Biochemistry

## Official scope

BIOC 202 is a 3-credit introduction to proteins and enzymes, carbohydrate metabolism, and glucose homeostasis, with emphasis on human biochemistry and health-science applications.

## Main course content

- Amino-acid and protein structure at an introductory level
- Enzymes, catalysis, and regulation
- Carbohydrate structure and metabolism
- Glycolysis and related energy pathways
- Glycogen storage and mobilization
- Gluconeogenesis
- Regulation of blood glucose and glucose homeostasis
- Relationships between biochemical pathways and human physiology

## Prerequisite context

The official prerequisite is one of CHEM 203, CHEM 223, or CHEM 233.
