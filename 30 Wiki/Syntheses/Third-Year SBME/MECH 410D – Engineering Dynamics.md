# MECH 410D – Engineering Dynamics

## Official scope

The SBME technical-elective table identifies MECH 410D as a 3-credit Engineering Dynamics course. The UBC Academic Calendar lists MECH 410 more generally as variable-credit Special Topics in Mechanical Engineering, consisting of lectures and readings on specialized topics of current interest. It does not currently provide a section-specific 410D description.

## Provisional content boundary

Because the official section description is unavailable, no detailed topic list is treated as confirmed. Engineering-dynamics offerings may involve subjects such as:

- Three-dimensional particle or rigid-body kinematics
- Force–acceleration relationships
- Work–energy methods
- Impulse–momentum methods
- Angular momentum
- Coupled translation and rotation
- Equations of motion
- Dynamic-system modelling

These are reasonable possibilities based on the course title, not verified MECH 410D coverage.
