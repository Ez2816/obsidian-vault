# MATH 253 – Multivariable Calculus

## Purpose of the course

MATH 253 builds local and integral reasoning for functions with several variables and spatially distributed quantities. This page is a conceptual map, not a reconstruction of the official syllabus.

## Main conceptual units

Functions of Several Variables; Partial Derivatives; Directional Derivatives; Gradient; Tangent Planes and Linear Approximation; Multivariable Extrema; Second Derivative Test in Multiple Variables; Lagrange Multipliers; Double and Triple Integrals; Coordinate Changes in Multiple Integrals.
