# MATH 264 – Vector Calculus for Electrical Engineering

## Purpose of the course

MATH 264 provides the field, flux, circulation, and integral-theorem language used in electromagnetics and transport. This page is a conceptual map, not a reconstruction of the official syllabus.

## Main conceptual units

Scalar and Vector Fields; Gradient and Directional Change; Divergence; Curl; Line Integrals and Circulation; Surface Integrals and Flux; Gauss's Theorem; Stokes' Theorem; Why Electromagnetic Integrals Can Be Negative.
