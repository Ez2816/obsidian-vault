# MATH 256 – Differential Equations

## Purpose of the course

MATH 256 models time- and space-dependent systems using ordinary and partial differential equations and transform methods. This page is a conceptual map, not a reconstruction of the official syllabus.

## Main conceptual units

Differential Equations as Models; First-Order Linear Differential Equations; Second-Order Linear Differential Equations; Systems of Differential Equations; Laplace Transforms; Fourier Series; Separation of Variables for Linear PDEs; Initial vs Boundary Conditions; Diffusion and Heat Equations.
