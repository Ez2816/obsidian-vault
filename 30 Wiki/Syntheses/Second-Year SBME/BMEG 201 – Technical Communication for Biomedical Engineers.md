# BMEG 201 – Technical Communication for Biomedical Engineers

## Purpose of the course

BMEG 201 develops evidence-based technical communication for engineering decisions, reports, instructions, correspondence, and presentations. This page is a conceptual map, not a reconstruction of the official syllabus.

## Main conceptual units

Audience, Purpose, and Technical Context; Technical Reports; Recommendation Memos; Business Correspondence for Engineers; Writing Negative Business Messages; Writing Instructions for Use; Warnings vs Precautions; Oral Technical Presentations.
