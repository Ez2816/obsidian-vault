# BMEG 210 – Thermodynamics in Biomedical Engineering

## Purpose of the course

BMEG 210 uses conservation laws and thermodynamic reasoning to analyze matter, energy, directionality, and useful work in engineered and biological systems. This page is a conceptual map, not a reconstruction of the official syllabus.

## Main conceptual units

Thermodynamic Systems, Boundaries, and States; Heat, Work, and Energy; Material Balances; Energy Balances; First Law of Thermodynamics; Enthalpy and Fluid Properties; Second Law and Entropy; Reversible vs Irreversible Processes; Exergy and Availability; Hess's Law; Thermodynamics of Biomedical Systems.
